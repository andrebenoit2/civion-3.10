Campaign Agreement

This agreement outlines the terms and conditions for political campaigns utilizing Civion's AI-powered textbanking platform.

Campaign Responsibilities

  • Maintain compliance with all applicable campaign finance laws and regulations
  • Ensure accurate representation of campaign positions and messaging
  • Provide truthful and up-to-date campaign information
  • Monitor and moderate all automated communications

Platform Usage

  • Use the platform exclusively for legitimate campaign communications
  • Maintain accurate records of all communications and interactions
  • Adhere to best practices for voter engagement and outreach
  • Report any technical issues or concerns promptly
